# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## Quality Assurance (QA) Engineers

### Role Summary
QA Engineers ensure software quality through testing strategy, test execution, and defect tracking. They work closely with developers and product managers to validate that features meet requirements and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Design and execute test plans (manual and automated)
- Identify, document, and track defects
- Define acceptance criteria with product managers
- Maintain test automation frameworks
- Perform regression, integration, and performance testing
- Participate in sprint planning and retrospectives
- Collaborate with developers during code reviews to identify testability improvements

### Goals
- Prevent defects from reaching production
- Improve test coverage and automation
- Reduce time to detect and resolve issues
- Ensure features meet defined acceptance criteria

### Typical Communication
- Test results and defect reports in daily standups
- Quality metrics dashboards shared with project managers
- Collaboration with developers in PR reviews and bug triage
- Test plan reviews with product managers

---

## DevOps Engineers

### Role Summary
DevOps Engineers build and maintain infrastructure, CI/CD pipelines, and deployment processes. They enable reliable, automated software delivery and ensure system reliability.

### Responsibilities
- Design and maintain CI/CD pipelines
- Manage cloud infrastructure and environments
- Implement monitoring and alerting systems
- Automate deployment and rollback processes
- Ensure security and compliance in infrastructure
- Support incident response and troubleshooting
- Collaborate with developers on build and deployment optimization

### Goals
- Enable fast, safe deployments
- Maximize system uptime and reliability
- Automate repetitive operational tasks
- Reduce deployment cycle time

### Typical Communication
- Infrastructure status reports to project managers
- Deployment schedules coordinated with developers and product managers
- Incident reports and post-mortems
- Capacity planning and performance metrics
- Runbooks and operational documentation

---

## UX/UI Designers

### Role Summary
UX/UI Designers create user-centered designs that balance user needs with business goals. They research, prototype, and validate design solutions before and during development.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct user research and usability testing
- Create wireframes, mockups, and prototypes
- Define design systems and UI patterns
- Collaborate with developers on implementation feasibility
- Validate designs with users and metrics
- Work with product managers to refine requirements based on user feedback

### Goals
- Deliver intuitive, accessible user experiences
- Reduce user friction and support costs
- Maintain design consistency across products
- Validate design decisions with data and user feedback

### Typical Communication
- Design specs and prototypes shared with developers and product managers
- User research findings presented to stakeholders
- Usability reports and design validation results
- Design reviews with product and engineering teams
- Collaboration in sprint planning to scope design work

---

## Scrum Masters / Agile Coaches

### Role Summary
Scrum Masters facilitate agile processes and remove impediments to team productivity. They coach teams on agile practices and ensure ceremonies are effective.

### Responsibilities
- Facilitate sprint ceremonies (standups, planning, retros)
- Remove blockers and impediments
- Coach team on agile principles and practices
- Track team velocity and process metrics
- Shield team from distractions and context switching
- Work with project managers to align on delivery commitments
- Foster collaboration between developers, QA, and other team members

### Goals
- Improve team velocity and predictability
- Foster continuous improvement culture
- Maintain sustainable pace and team health
- Ensure effective communication across all roles

### Typical Communication
- Sprint reports and burndown charts shared with project managers
- Retrospective action items tracked and communicated
- Escalation of blockers to project managers and leadership
- Facilitation of cross-functional team discussions
- Process improvement proposals

---

## Technical Writers

### Role Summary
Technical Writers create and maintain documentation for developers, users, and stakeholders. They ensure information is clear, accurate, and accessible.

### Responsibilities
- Write and maintain API documentation, user guides, and runbooks
- Collaborate with developers and DevOps engineers on technical accuracy
- Organize and structure documentation repositories
- Create tutorials and getting-started guides
- Maintain documentation standards and templates
- Work with product managers to document features and release notes

### Goals
- Reduce support burden through self-service documentation
- Enable faster onboarding and adoption
- Ensure documentation stays current with releases
- Improve documentation discoverability and usability

### Typical Communication
- Documentation updates included in release notes with product managers
- Collaboration with developers during feature development for technical specs
- User feedback sessions to improve documentation clarity
- Reviews with QA engineers to ensure accuracy of troubleshooting guides
- Coordination with project managers on documentation timelines

---

## Security Engineers

### Role Summary
Security Engineers protect systems and data through security architecture, threat modeling, and vulnerability management. They embed security practices throughout the development lifecycle.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct security reviews and threat modeling
- Perform vulnerability scanning and penetration testing
- Define security standards and best practices
- Respond to security incidents
- Train teams on secure coding practices
- Manage compliance and audit requirements
- Collaborate with developers on security fixes and DevOps engineers on infrastructure security

### Goals
- Prevent security vulnerabilities and breaches
- Ensure compliance with security standards
- Build security into development processes
- Minimize time to detect and respond to security issues

### Typical Communication
- Security assessment reports shared with project managers and developers
- Vulnerability tracking and remediation plans coordinated with developers and QA
- Security advisories and incident reports to stakeholders
- Training sessions for developers on secure coding
- Compliance reports and audit documentation
- Collaboration with DevOps engineers on infrastructure security controls
